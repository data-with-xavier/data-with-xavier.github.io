Your Resume Isn't The Problem. (You Are)

What Aspiring Analysts Get Wrong About Getting Hired

You did what you were told. You learned the tools. You built the projects. You applied everywhere. And nothing happened.

The version of analytics you prepared for no longer exists. AI generates SQL, exploratory analysis, and dashboards in seconds. What AI cannot do is think — ask the right questions, frame the real problem, communicate a clear "so what" to someone about to act on it. That's what hiring managers are actually evaluating.

This book is built around ten things that separate analysts who get hired from those who don't. Not tools. Not certifications. The ten things that signal you understand the job, can handle the ambiguity, and are worth the risk of an offer.
