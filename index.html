About Me

XC

I’m Xavier Conzet — an analytics engineer who’s spent his career chasing one question: why do some analytics efforts actually change how organizations operate, while others just produce reports nobody reads?

At the University of St. Thomas, I’m a Corporate Fellow teaching data visualization and storytelling. My course covers the fundamentals — the right chart, the right structure, the right visual — but the deeper focus is on what most programs skip: how to build a narrative from data that earns trust and moves people to action. The gap between "the data says X" and "so we’re going to do Y" is where most analytics work quietly dies. That’s what I teach people to close.

I’m also writing a book on what the analytics field is becoming — what employers actually need, what the job looks like today versus five years ago, and what it genuinely takes to build a career in it. Most people entering the field are working off an outdated map. I want to give them a better one.

If you want to talk data, trade ideas, or explore working together — I’d love to connect.
